Chocolate Cake

⏱️ 30 min
👥 6 servings
📊 Medium
0%
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Cocoa Powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • Butter
  • Baking Powder
  1. Mix all dry ingredients.
  2. Add eggs and milk.

  4. Pour into baking tray.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Allow cake to cool.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Homemade Ice Cream

⏱️ 240 min
👥 8 servings
📊 Easy
0%
  • 2 Cups Heavy Cream
  • 1 Cup Whole Milk
  • ¾ Cup Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Toppings of your choice
  1. Whisk sugar into milk until dissolved.
  2. Add heavy cream and vanilla extract.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and mix well.
  4. Pour mixture into ice cream maker.
  5. Churn for 20–25 minutes until thick.
  6. Transfer to a container and freeze for 4 hours.
  7. Scoop, add toppings, and enjoy!

Chicken Biryani

⏱️ 60 min
👥 4 servings
📊 Hard
0%
  • 500g Chicken (bone-in)
  • 2 Cups Basmati Rice (soaked)
  • 2 Onions (thinly sliced, fried)
  • 1 Cup Yogurt
  • Biryani Masala (2 tbsp)
  • Saffron strands in warm milk
  • Ghee, Ginger-Garlic paste
  • Fresh Mint & Cilantro
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cinnamon)
  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, masala & ginger-garlic paste for 30 min.
  2. Parboil basmati rice with whole spices until 70% cooked. Drain.
  3. Fry sliced onions until deep golden brown. Set aside.
  4. Layer marinated chicken at the bottom of a heavy pot.
  5. Add a layer of rice, fried onions, mint, cilantro & saffron milk.
  6. Seal the pot with dough or foil and cook on low heat (dum) for 25 min.
  7. Gently mix, garnish with fried onions & serve hot!

🍟 Snack

Crispy French Fries

⏱️ 25 min
👥 4 servings
📊 Easy
0%
  • 4 Large Potatoes
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Paprika (optional)
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Ketchup for serving
  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Soak in cold water for 30 minutes. Drain and pat dry.
  3. Heat oil to 300°F (150°C). Fry strips for 5 min (blanch fry). Remove.
  4. Increase oil to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Fry again for 2–3 minutes until golden and crispy.
  6. Drain on paper towels. Season with salt immediately.
  7. Serve hot with ketchup!
